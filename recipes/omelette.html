Omelette

The best way to start your day is with a delicious omelette

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • a pinch of salt
  • a pinch of black pepper
  • 15g mushrooms
  • 15g green peppers
  • 15g grated cheese
  • 15g butter

Steps

  1. Beat the eggs and season with salt and pepper.
  2. Heat the butter in a non-stick frying pan on medium-low heat.
  3. Pour the eggs into the pan with the mushrooms, peppers and cheese.
  4. Let the mixture cook for 2 minutes.
  5. Carefully flip the omelette over to the other side and cook for anothe 2 minutes.
  6. Slide on to a plate and serve.
